The Lehigh Safety Pledge

We pledge to provide the highest level of safety and service and follow all CDC, company, state and local guidelines during our Ordering & Wellness Events.

During The Event We Will

Have a supply of masks and gloves

Clean footwear samples with disinfectant wipes or spray after each use

Wear safety glasses with side shields and earplugs

Wear booties or rubber overshoes over our safety footwear

Use hand sanitizer and wash our hands throughout the day

Supply stylus pens stored in alcohol to use with touch screen monitors

Use alcohol/disinfectant wipes to clean our equipment after each use

Provide small trash cans with liners for disposal of wipes and other materials

Require all of your employees to use provided hand sanitizer upon arrival

Before We Leave We Will

Consolidate all small trash bags into one large bag and dispose

Clean all used tables and chairs with alcohol/disinfectant wipes

Spray entire event area with disinfectant
